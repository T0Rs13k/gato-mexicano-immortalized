Gato

This is a special variant which consists of gold-plated steak inside. The products here are Gatacos, Gawarmas, and Gachos.

Mexican

This is also a special variant which consists of Pollo (chicken), Cerdo (pork), and Carne (beef). We have the Mexi"Ta"cos, Mexiwarmas, and Mexichos.

Pollo

Pollo means chicken in English. Which means we have Pollacos (chicken tacos), Pollarmas (chicken shawarmas), and Pollchos (chicken nachos).

Cerdo

Here we have Cerdo. Cerdo means pork in English. Which means we have Cerdacos (pork tacos), Cerdamas (pork shawarmas), and Cerchos (pork nachos).

Carne

Last but not the least, we have Carne. Carne means beef in English. The products here are Carnacos (beef tacos), Cawarmas (beef shawarmas), and Carnachos (beef nachos).
