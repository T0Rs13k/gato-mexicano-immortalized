History

At August 13, 2021, We have been thinking for a name and the content of our business. So we decided to make a Mexican restaurant. The first concept of the name was Mexicat because we decided that adding the cat could make the name interesting. After making the name, we decided to make the tagline, which is “The future of Mexico”. Then somehow, we thought that Mexicat may be a bad name, so we decided to change the name and translate “Mexican Cat” into Spanish, and now our restaurant name is Gato Mexicano.
