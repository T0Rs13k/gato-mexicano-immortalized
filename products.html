Shawarma

Shawarma is a popular Levantine dish consisting of meat cut into thin slices, stacked in a cone-like shape, and roasted on a slowly-turning vertical rotisserie or spit. The flavors of this product are chicken, pork, beef, kebab, tikka, and queso.

Burrito
₱220

A burrito is a dish in Mexican and Tex-Mex cuisine that took form in California cuisine, consisting of a flour tortilla wrapped into a sealed cylindrical shape around various ingredients. The flavors of this product are chicken, pork, beef, all meat, and gold-plated steak.

A quesadilla is a Mexican dish consisting of a tortilla that is filled primarily with cheese, and sometimes meats, spices, and other fillings, and then cooked on a griddle or stove. The flavors of this product are chicken, pork, and beef.
